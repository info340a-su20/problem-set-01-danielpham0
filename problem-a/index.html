About Uncle Iroh

"While it is always best to believe in oneself, a little help from others can be a great blessing."

Uncle Iroh is a firebending master from the Fire Nation, who's main objective is to guide his nephew Zuko. In the show, Avatar the Last Airbender, he consistently uses his wisdom and age to help others with their problems. Though a well respected bender, he is also known for his love of tea, depicted in the image above.

Some things I admire about this character:

  • His humor.
  • His wisdom.
  • His wholesomeness.
